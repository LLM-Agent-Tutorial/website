Enhancing Reasoning Capabilities

Reasoning lies behind planning, tool use, and cooperation. The frontier section of the paper focuses on eliciting explicit thought chains, exploring many candidate traces at inference time, and training agents with reinforcement signals.

Reasoning boosters

Stack these techniques to improve reliability: prompt for intermediate thoughts, allocate more inference budget to explore, and align the reasoning policy by optimizing against trusted rewards.

  • Chain-of-thought prompts: Explicit cues like “Let’s think step by step” or providing reference traces encourage deliberate decomposition. Variants such as self-consistency and Tree-of-Thoughts let the agent explore multiple reasoning branches before answering.
  • Test-time scaling: Beam search, Monte Carlo Tree Search, or majority vote over diverse traces increase accuracy without changing model weights. Keeping the most consistent outputs works especially well for planning-heavy agents.
  • Reinforcement learning: Algorithms such as REINFORCE, PPO, DPO, or GRPO optimize the entire reasoning chain using programmatic rewards, verified outcomes, or ranked human preferences so that good intermediate steps are reinforced.
