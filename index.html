Abstract

Why this tutorial matters

This tutorial is designed for researchers and engineers who are interested in large language model (LLM)-based agents but have little or no prior experience with LLMs, machine learning, artificial intelligence, or programming. It offers a gentle yet comprehensive introduction, providing newcomers with an intuitive, high-level understanding of what LLM agents can do, while also serving as a practical guide for those who wish to explore how these agents actually work.

Key takeaways include: (i) foundational concepts of LLMs and LLM-based agents; (ii) a curated set of nine concrete examples demonstrating essential agent capabilities and the mechanisms behind them across diverse domains; (iii) practical tips for developing LLM agents; and (iv) a brief roadmap of advanced techniques such as injecting domain knowledge, improving reasoning abilities, and managing long context.

3 · Practical tips

Keep designs simple, define goals clearly, guide with human workflows, and rely on example-driven prompts.

4 · Advanced frontier

Inject domain knowledge, strengthen reasoning, manage long context, internalize agent skills, and keep agents safe.
